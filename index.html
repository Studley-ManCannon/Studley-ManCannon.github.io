Who Am I?

Hello potential employer or random visitor. I'm Cole Campbell, a computer scientist with interests in game development, AI, and all other forms of programming. I'm currently working towards a Bachelor's in Computer Science at Loughborough University. If you'd like to see a handful of projects I've either developed myself or have helped work on, check out my "Projects" section below. Aside from programming, I also assist in the moderation of an online community known as OWLcord (a discord server for Overwatch esports) which currently has 4000 members. I enjoy playing a wide variety of games such as Destiny 2, Divinity: Original Sin 2, Overwatch, various Pokemon games, Minecraft, and Civilisation V.
